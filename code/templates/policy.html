Tenant Policy

General Rules and Guidelines

- Rent Payment: Rent is due on the 1st of each month. Late payments may incur additional fees.
- Noise: Quiet hours are from 10 PM to 7 AM. Please be considerate of your neighbors.
- Overnight guests are limited to 3 consecutive nights and no more than 10 nights per month.
- Maintenance: Report any maintenance issues promptly to the management.
- Cleanliness: Keep your living space and common areas clean and tidy.
- Smoking: This is a non-smoking property. Smoking is prohibited inside the building.
- Pets: Pets are allowed with prior approval and may require an additional deposit.
- Alterations: No alterations to the property without written permission from management.
- Common Areas: Respect shared spaces and follow posted guidelines for their use.
- Security: Do not share building access codes or keys with non-residents.

Additional Information

For full details on policies and procedures, please refer to your lease agreement or contact the management office.
